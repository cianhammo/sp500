About This Project

I am fascinated by both market dynamics and LLMs, so I built this tool over the course of a week to experiment at their intersection.

How It Works

At market close each day, this tool:

  • Analyzes the S&P 500 stock movements
  • Identifies the day's best and worst performers and analyses relevant data
  • Searches the web for relevant information on why these stocks performed so unusually
  • Uses an LLM to sumarize the information and generate insights

Future Development

Planned features include:

  • Sentiment analysis of financial subreddits and social media to track market sentiment
  • Looking for correlation analysis between prediction markets and traditional markets (e.g. look for correlations between this market predicting the outcome of the Russo-Ukrainian War, and the forex of the Rouble)
