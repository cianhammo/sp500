About This Project

I am fascinated by both market dynamics and LLMs, so I built this tool over the course of a week to experiment at their intersection.

How It Works

At market close each day, this tool:

  • Analyzes the S&P 500 stock movements
  • Identifies the day's best and worst performers and analyses relevant data
  • Searches the web for relevant information on why these stocks performed so unusually
  • Uses an LLM to sumarize the information and explain insights
flowchart TD FIN[Finance API\n e.g. TSLA: -8%] --> A[Python Statistics\n RSI: 78\nVolatility: 68% above 30-day avg] FIN --> B["Google Search API Search: Tesla news in the last 24 hours Result: The president has announced that electric cars are to face a tougher regulatory environment due to..."] A --> C[LLM API] B --> C C --> OUT['Tesla shares dropped 8% following the announcement of stricter EV \n regulations. Technical indicators are looking bearish, with the stock in\n overbought territory and showing unusually high volatility.']

Future Development
